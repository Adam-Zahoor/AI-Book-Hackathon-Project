# Physical AI and Robotics: Introduction

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on Physical AI and Robotics. This book provides a complete pathway for becoming a highly capable AI and Robotics developer, guiding you from zero knowledge to professional-level competence.

## About This Book

This book combines theory, practical skills, hands-on robotics, and AI engineering best practices. It's designed to help students, teenagers, and aspiring developers build intelligent systems that interact with the physical world.

### What You'll Learn

- Fundamentals of AI, ML, robotics, and embedded systems
- How to combine AI models with robotics hardware
- Best practices for safe, ethical, responsible, and secure AI/robotics development
- How to build full AI-native projects, from small automation tasks to autonomous robots

### Target Audience

This book is designed for:
- Beginners with zero knowledge
- Teenagers and students interested in AI or robotics
- Developers who want to transition into AI engineering
- Hobbyists building physical robots or automation systems
- Researchers looking for a structured foundation

Let's begin our journey into the fascinating world of Physical AI and Robotics.
